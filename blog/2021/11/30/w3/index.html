Progress

This week as a team, we decided to familiarise ourselves with React, and creating components with HTML and CSS. This meant that a lot of our efforts lay in watching videos and tutorials in order to make some example components for our future webpage.

Why React?

We chose react as it is a widely used frame work for websites, and although we have not used it before, we believe it would be good to grasp and use to create the website in the long run.

Why Creating Components?

The creation of components ensures that when creating new pages on our website, they are similar in theme and look more uniform, increasing the usability of our website for nurses and the admin who need to edit the pages.
