Login Page

In Week 5 and the Christmas break, we started to implement the login system for the website. Here we made user groups for the clinical staff, and log in pages for them as well as the hospital and trust admins. Implementing the log in at this stage was necessary to build upon our app and to manage what certain users of the kit could use. After logging in, the user was taking to the home page with various buttons and icons to showcase the different pages to navigate towards.

Home Page

To implement the user groups, the database was also developed. Having a central database allows multiple logins to be stored in one place, reducing the need to create individual systems across trusts and making the program more extendable.

Equipment Table

This led us to start working on the equipment section of the kit. After the equipment table was configured in the database, equipment found at the hospital was displayed through a table. We chose to use a table as it provides a clear view of the equipment stored at each hospital, whilst also providing admins with a suitable way to manage equipment as a whole rather than individually, especially for the mass editing of potential equipment.
