Sharing Page

Over these two weeks, we worked as a group to gain more of an understanding with our client in regards to sharing data regarding equipment. After meeting with them, we understood they desired a more email like system. This led to initial front end work to create this, and development into a sharing system to embed such equipment for sharing between hospitals. Here is an initial view of our sharing system.

User Groups

The login system had been complete to a prototype level, with 2 main user groups, administrators and clinical staff. The admins are split into two groups - the hospital admins and the trust admins, the latter having the most privileges. In addition to this, new inboxes have been added in order to add to the sharing sub-problem above.
