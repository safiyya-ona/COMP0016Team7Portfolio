Abstract

As Clinical Staff work in the hospital, they are met with a large range of equipment with various methods of usages. Due to to the sheer amount of equipment, staff may forget how to use equipment - this could potentially lead to adverse consequences. This is where our solution comes in.

The Clinical QR Kit is our solution to this problem - creating a web application that utilises QR code for ease of access ensures that staff can quickly get to work on the equipment. The website is filled with a wide range of resources regarding the equipment. For admin users, other key features such as equipment sharing, equipment viewings and issues can also be managed within the system.

We are glad to say that this project was a success, having achieved all requirements. We have received positive feedback from our client and other users, highlighting the flexibility and usefulness of our solution.
